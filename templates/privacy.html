Privacy

Short, clear version of how CareerAI handles your data.

What you paste

We do not scrape any websites. You paste job descriptions, internship descriptions, and referral context into CareerAI. Those inputs are used only to run the tools you request (Job Pack, Internship Analyzer, Referral Trainer, etc.).

Resume & Profile Portal

Resume storage is optional and can be deleted. Your Profile Portal and Resume Profile are used to power Skill Mapper, Portfolio Builder, Job Packs and other tools inside CareerAI. They are not shown publicly unless you explicitly enable a public portfolio page.

Public portfolio pages

Public portfolio pages only render the content you configure plus minimal CareerAI branding. They do not expose dashboards, credit data, or internal analytics. You choose when to publish or unpublish.

University / tenant visibility

If you access CareerAI through your university portal, admins at your university may see aggregate usage analytics (for example, total runs of Skill Mapper or Job Packs). They do not see your private tool inputs or AI outputs, unless your institution has a separate agreement that clearly states otherwise.

Billing & payments

Payments are handled entirely by Stripe. CareerAI does not store or process your card details. We only retain minimal billing metadata (for example, that your account is on Pro) so that features like Gold ⭐ credits work correctly.

Deleting your data

You can request deletion of your account data via support or your university administrator. We will remove your Profile Portal, Resume Profile, and any stored AI outputs associated with your account, subject to legal and compliance requirements.

This is a high-level summary. If your university or institution has a separate data-processing agreement with CareerAI, that document will take priority where there is any conflict.
